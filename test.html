Channelling over 30 years of expertise into the pursuit of ultimate skin wellness, Elemis' motto is: 'defined by nature, led by science'. This carefully crafted skin care formulas combine cutting-edge patented technology with nature's finest active extracts to deliver results-driven treatments - like the Superfood Midnight Facial. This decadent overnight cream is packed with nutrient-dense superfoods to pamper skin as you snooze, revealing healthy, glowing skin come morning.

Introducing Elemis' Superfood Midnight Facial, a rich night cream formulated to quench thirsty skin with a hydrating mix of superfoods and natural extracts.

Awaken to a juicy, 'just left the spa' radiance thanks to a clever cocktail of nourishing ingredients including kale, goji berries, avocado and broccoli - basically a salad for your face. These superfoods are supplemented with camu camu, a powerful antioxidant that is rich in vitamin C, helping to brighten dull skin and smooth fine lines and texture. To really ramp up hydration levels, there's also cocoa butter, which contains fatty acids that flood skin with essential moisture, for a soft, plumped up appearance.

Aqua/Water/Eau, PEG-8, Glycerin, Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Prunus Amygdalus Dulcis (Sweet Almond) Oil, Dicaprylyl Carbonate, Sodium Polyacrylate, Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter, Hydroxyacetophenone, Phenoxyethanol, Theobroma Cacao (Cocoa) Seed Butter, Fragrance (Parfum), Carbomer, Disodium EDTA, Alpha-Glucan Oligosaccharide, Bentonite, Chondrus Crispus (Carrageenan) Extract, Passiflora Edulis Seed Oil, Limonene, Citrus Aurantium Dulcis (Orange) Peel Oil, Propanediol, Glyceryl Acrylate/Acrylic Acid Copolymer, Linalool, Benzyl Alcohol, Citronellol, Lithothamnion Calcareum Extract, Sodium Hydroxide, Theobroma Cacao (Cocoa) Seed Extract, Litsea Cubeba Fruit Oil, Citrus Aurantifolia (Lime) Oil, Musa Sapientum (Banana) Flower Extract, Citral, Myrciaria Dubia Fruit Extract, Tocopherol, Dehydroacetic Acid, Sodium Benzoate, Citric Acid, Potassium Sorbate.

For the latest information, it is recommended to review the ingredient list printed on the packaging of the product prior to usage or consumption.

To be used every evening before bed. After cleansing and toning, warm a pea sized amount between the palms and gently glaze over the face. Always use upward, stroking, sweeping motions when applying moisturiser to the face.

Avoid direct contact with eyes.
